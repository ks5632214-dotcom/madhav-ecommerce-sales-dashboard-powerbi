## **1Ô∏è. Project Overview**

The Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability trends, customer behavior, and product insights.

This dashboard transforms raw ecommerce transactional data into actionable business insights to support strategic decision-making.

## **2Ô∏è. Business Problem**

Ecommerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data. However, without structured analysis, it is difficult to identify:

- High-performing states.  
- Profitable product categories.  
- Seasonal profit trends.  
- Customer purchasing patterns.  
- Preferred payment methods.  

Raw data alone does not provide clear decision-making insights.

## **3Ô∏è. Project Objective**

The objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales and profit performance.  
- Identify profit-driving and loss-making months.  
- Highlight top-performing customers and regions.  
- Analyze category and sub-category contribution. 
- Support data-driven business decisions.

## **6Ô∏è. Key Insights**

- Maharashtra generates the highest revenue.
- Clothing contributes 63% of total quantity sold.
- Printers are the most profitable sub-category.
- November is the most profitable month.
- Losses are observed in May, July, September, and December.
- COD is the most preferred payment method (44%).
